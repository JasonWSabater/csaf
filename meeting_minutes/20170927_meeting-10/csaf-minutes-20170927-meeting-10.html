8. CSAF Documentation Website and Open Repository

https://www.oasis-open.org/resources/open-repositories

It is required that a TC identifies Initial Maintainers for such repository:
Initial Maintainer(s): The proposal should identify one or more TC members willing and otherwise qualified to serve as initial Maintainer(s) for any Open Repository. Ideally, such person(s) would have substantial experience with GitHub or similar distributed version control system. Alternatively, if the TC members have informal commitment from someone outside the TC who could serve as the key technical lead, plans could be drafted to approve that person as lead Maintainer once the project has been launched.

Omar: Stefan Hagen, Vincent Danen, and Omar Santos have volunteered to help with the creation of the CSAF documentation website, and they can be the initial maintainers of the open repository.

Harold: I move to approve Stefan Hagen, Vincent Danen, and Omar Santos as the CSAF Open Repository Initial Maintainers. Troy seconds it.

Omar: Unanimous consent, the motion carries, CSAF Open Repository Initial Maintainers approved.
